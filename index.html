We have targeted our website to help out kids who have lost their schools to the recent conflict between Ukraine and Russia. This webstite is designed to help these kids learn about math in a fun way. Through competitions, tutorials embeded within the website, and online tutoring we hope to develop these childrens' love for math. Although this is targeted for kids who have lost their schools due to the recent conflicts, anyone who wants to use it to review or to learn can use it as it is a site to help others learn.
